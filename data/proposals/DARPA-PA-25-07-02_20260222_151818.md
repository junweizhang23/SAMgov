## 2. Technical Approach

### Understanding of the Problem  
- Need for compositional integration of Automated Reasoning (AR) and Machine Learning (ML) to build AI systems of systems with high assurance.  
- Challenges include scalability, verifiability, adaptability to evolving conditions, and broad applicability across complex engineering domains.  
- Importance of foundational research to enable automated composition of verified components with learned behaviors.

### Proposed Solution Architecture  
- **Hybrid AR+ML Framework:** Develop a modular architecture combining formal reasoning engines (e.g., SMT solvers, theorem provers) with advanced ML models (deep neural nets, probabilistic models).  
- **Compositional Verification Layer:** Implement automated techniques for verifying properties of individual AI components and their compositions.  
- **Adaptive Learning Module:** Employ continual learning to refine models based on system feedback while ensuring safety and correctness constraints.  
- **Interoperability Middleware:** Design interfaces and protocols for seamless interaction between AR and ML components and external systems.  
- **Prototype Implementation & Demonstration:** Build proof-of-concept system validating core compositional learning and reasoning capabilities.

### Innovation Elements  
- Novel integration methods for symbolic reasoning and ML that preserve formal guarantees while enabling learning flexibility.  
- Use of advanced AI explainability techniques to enhance transparency and trustworthiness.  
- Employing cloud-native scalable computing resources for rapid experimentation and iterative refinement.  
- Leveraging domain-agnostic compositional verification methods to ensure broad applicability.

### Risk Mitigation  
- Early-stage feasibility study to validate core integration concepts with incremental milestones.  
- Parallel development of fallback verification strategies to reduce dependence on immature methods.  
- Use of rigorous test suites and simulation environments for continuous validation.  
- Engagement with DARPA technical points-of-contact for iterative feedback and risk management.
